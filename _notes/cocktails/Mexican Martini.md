## Ingredients

- 2 oz reposado tequila
- 1 oz Cointreau
- 1 oz lime juice
- ¾ oz orange juice
- ½ oz olive brine
- 2 olives for garnish
- Coarse (margarita) salt

## Instructions

1. Wet half the lip of a glass and dip it in salt. Chill the glass.
2. Pour the liquid ingredients into a cocktail shaker and fill with ice.
3. Shake vigorously for 10 seconds.
4. Strain into the glass and garnish with olives.
