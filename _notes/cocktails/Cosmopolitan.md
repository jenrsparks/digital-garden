## Ingredients

- 2 oz citrus vodka
- ¾ oz Cointreau
- 2 oz fresh lime juice
- ½ oz pure, unsweetened cranberry juice
- 2 oz simple syrup 
- Garnish: lime wheel

## Instructions

1. Shake all the ingredients with ice.
2. Double strain into a chilled coupe.
3. Garnish with the lime wheel.

## Variation: Blood Orange

- 2 oz blood orange vodka
- ¾ oz Cointreau
- 2 oz fresh lime juice
- ½ oz orange juice
- ¾ oz simple syrup
