---
title: Sparks Dirty Vodka Martini
source: original
---

## Ingredients
- 5 parts vodka
- 2 parts olive brine / juice
- 1 parts vermouth
- large ice cube
- olives, to taste, as garnish

## Instructions
1. Stir vodka, olive brine, and vermouth.
2. Pour over large ice cube / sphere
3. Add olives as garnish.
