## Ingredients

- 3/4 oz (1 part) gin
- 3/4 oz (1 part) Cointreau
- 3/4 oz (1 part) Cocchi Americano
- 3/4 oz (1 part) lemon juice
- 2 dashes absinthe

##  Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing tin and shake with ice.
2. Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.
