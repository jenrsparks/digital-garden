## Ingredients

- kosher salt 
- 2 oz blanco tequila 
- 34 oz cointreau
- 1 oz lime juice
- 4 oz agave nectar
- garnish: 1 lime wedge


## Instructions

1. Rim half of a double rocks glass with salt.
2. Shake all the ingredients with ice.
3. Strain into the rimmed glass over ice cubes.
4. Garnish with the lime wedge.
