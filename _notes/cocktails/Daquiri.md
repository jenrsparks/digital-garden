## Ingredients

_Makes ~4 oz drink_ 

- 2 oz (8 parts) white rum
- 1 oz (4 parts) ‎lime juice
- ¾ oz (3 parts) simple (1:1) syrup
- lime wedge for garnish

## Instructions
1. Shake ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into coup glass.
3. Garnish with lime wedge.

## Variants

- Add 1/2 oz (2 parts) orange juice for more citrus zing
